Somewhere in Pirin

The Pirin Mountains (Bulgarian: Пирин) are a mountain range in southwestern Bulgaria, with Vihren at an altitude of 2,914 m being the highest peak.

The mountain is named after Perun (Bulgarian: Перун), the highest god of the Slavic pantheon and the god of thunder and lightning. In the Antiquity the range was called Perinthos by the Thracians, meaning "Rocky Mountain".
